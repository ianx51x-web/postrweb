What They All
Have in Common
🎯
They Simplify the Buy

Every top player makes the first purchase easier than the average med spa. Less menu, less pricing confusion, less hesitation. The path from "I'm interested" to "I'm booked" is frictionless by design — not by accident.

🔁
They Build Retention on Purpose

Face Foundrié improved membership length by 30% with perks and loyalty initiatives. Glowbar bakes in a 4-month commitment. Retention isn't an afterthought here — it's engineered into the model from day one.

LaserAway doesn't sell vague "beauty." Peachy built entirely around wrinkle treatment. None of them try to do everything. Narrow is easier to scale, easier to market, and much easier to win with.

They Act Like Brands, Not Clinics

Consistent experience, standardized trust signals, cleaner websites, a clear point of view. That's why they outgrow local med spas still running on one injector, one front desk, and an Instagram page.
